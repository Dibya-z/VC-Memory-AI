# VaultGuard — Seed Pitch Deck

**Tagline:** Non-human identity security — discover, govern, and rotate every secret, token, and machine credential.

## The problem
Modern systems have far more machine identities (service accounts, API keys, OAuth tokens, certificates) than human ones — often 50:1. These "non-human identities" are sprawled across cloud, CI/CD, and SaaS, rarely rotated, and a leading cause of breaches. Security teams have almost no visibility into them.

## The solution
VaultGuard connects (read-only) to a company's cloud, code, and SaaS, then builds a live inventory of every non-human identity and secret: where it lives, what it can access, when it was last rotated, and whether it's exposed. It flags over-privileged and stale credentials and can automate rotation.

## Why now
- Several high-profile breaches in the last year started with a leaked machine token.
- The number of non-human identities is exploding with microservices and AI agents.
- Compliance frameworks are starting to require secrets governance.
