# SentinelKeys — Seed Pitch Deck

**Tagline:** Secure every machine identity — discover, govern, and rotate the secrets, tokens, and service accounts no one is watching.

## The problem
Modern systems have far more machine identities (API keys, service accounts, OAuth tokens, certificates) than human ones — often 50:1. They sprawl across cloud, CI/CD, and SaaS, are rarely rotated, and are a leading cause of breaches. Security teams have almost no visibility into them.

## The solution
SentinelKeys connects read-only to a company's cloud, code, and SaaS and builds a live inventory of every non-human identity and secret: where it lives, what it can access, when it was last rotated, and whether it's exposed. It flags over-privileged and stale credentials and can automate rotation.

## Why now
- Several recent breaches began with a leaked machine token.
- Machine identities are exploding with microservices and AI agents.
- Compliance frameworks increasingly require secrets governance.
