# NimbusHealth — Series A Pitch Deck

**Tagline:** Give every physician 90 minutes back a day — ambient AI that writes the note so doctors can focus on patients.

## The problem
Physicians spend up to 2 hours a day on documentation. It's the #1 driver of burnout and a direct cause of physicians leaving medicine. Patients feel it too: doctors stare at screens instead of at them.

## The solution
NimbusHealth is a fully ambient AI scribe. With patient consent, it listens to the visit, drafts the clinical note, suggests billing codes, and writes a structured summary straight into the EHR — no templates, no dictation commands.

## Why now
- Speech + medical-language models are finally accurate enough for real clinical use.
- Coding/reimbursement tailwinds reward cleaner documentation.
- Burnout is at an all-time high; health systems are actively buying solutions.

## Traction
- $2.1M ARR, ~15% MoM growth.
- 38 clinics live, 1,100 weekly active physicians.
- 94% clinic renewal rate; deep, native integrations with the two leading EHRs.
