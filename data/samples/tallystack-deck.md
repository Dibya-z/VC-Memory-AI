# TallyStack — Seed Pitch Deck

**Tagline:** Embedded billing & ledger for vertical SaaS — give your customers built-in accounting in a few API calls.

## The problem
Vertical SaaS platforms (for gyms, clinics, contractors) want to own the financial layer — invoicing, reconciliation, books — because it's sticky and high-margin. But building a correct, auditable double-entry ledger is hard and off-mission, so most bolt on a clunky QuickBooks integration instead.

## The solution
TallyStack is an embedded accounting API. Drop it in and your platform's customers get white-label invoicing, bank reconciliation, and a real double-entry ledger — multi-entity and multi-currency — without you building any of it.

## Why now
- Embedded finance is moving from payments into accounting/reconciliation.
- Vertical SaaS platforms are racing to add financial products to lift revenue per customer.

## Traction
- $700k ARR, growing ~16% month-over-month for the last six months.
- 16 platform customers; two contribute over $150k ARR each.
- Net revenue retention 128%; gross margin ~80%.

## Business model
Platform fee + per-active-end-user pricing, plus a small take rate on reconciled payment volume. Revenue compounds as customers' customers grow.
