NimbusHealth — founder call notes
Date: December 2025
Sector: Healthcare
Stage: Series A

Who: Dr. Elena Vasquez (CEO), Kwame Boateng (CTO). Quick 30-min follow-up call.

Vibe: Elena is impressive — clearly lived the documentation-burnout problem as a
practicing internist. Kwame is the real speech-AI depth. Good founder chemistry.

New things we learned:
- They just signed their first multi-site health system (8 clinics) — biggest deal
  to date, rolling out over Q1.
- Adding a real-time "coding assistant" that flags under-coding during the visit;
  early customers say it pays for the product on its own.

Concerns that came up (worth tracking):
- Small clinics are pushing back on the $299-399/seat price; may need a lighter tier.
- Month-6 physician retention is still unproven — clinic renewal is strong (94%)
  but we want to see individual doctors keep using it past the novelty period.
- Liability/malpractice exposure on AI-drafted notes — they have a human-review
  step, but indemnification policy is still being worked out with counsel.
- A couple of competitors with more funding are chasing the same health systems.

Strengths reinforced:
- The ROI story is genuinely strong (time saved + cleaner coding).
- Net revenue retention 121% — existing clinics expand fast.

Next step: partner meeting + reference calls with two live clinics before we decide.
